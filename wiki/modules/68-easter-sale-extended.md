## ⚠️ Warnings and Common Pitfalls

### For End Users
1. **Lifetime Plan Considerations**: "Lifetime" means the lifetime of the service, not the user. Companies can modify terms, discontinue services, or change ownership.
2. **Base64 Encoding**: The URL uses Base64 encoding (`aHR0cHM6Ly9sYW5kaW5nLnBjbG91ZC5jb20vRWFzdGVyMjAyNi1FWFQ=`), which is legitimate for tracking but can also be used maliciously in phishing attempts.
3. **Verify Sender**: Always confirm emails claiming to be from pCloud originate from official domains (`@pcloud.email`, `@pcloud.com`).

### For NBNE Operators
1. **Not Phishing**: Despite promotional language, this appears to be legitimate pCloud marketing
2. **User Education Opportunity**: Similar tactics used in phishing campaigns (urgency, limited-time offers)
3. **Domain Verification**: Email originates from `team@pcloud.email` which is a legitimate pCloud domain

## Verification Steps

If users question legitimacy:

1. **Check sender domain**: Confirm `@pcloud.email` alignment with known pCloud infrastructure
2. **Hover over links**: Verify they point to `pcloud.com` domains (don't click if suspicious)
3. **Visit directly**: Navigate to pCloud's official website independently rather than clicking email links
4. **Compare offers**: Check if the same promotion appears on pCloud's official marketing channels
