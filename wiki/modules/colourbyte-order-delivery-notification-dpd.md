# Colourbyte Order Delivery Notification (DPD)

This article covers the standard DPD delivery notification email that NBNE receives for Colourbyte equipment orders. These emails provide precise delivery windows, driver information, and tracking details. Understanding this notification format helps operators prepare for deliveries and coordinate with site security or reception staff accordingly.

## Email Identification

**Sender:** DPD (via Colourbyte order system)  
**Subject Format:** "Your Colourbyte order will be delivered today between [TIME] - [TIME]"  
**Typical Delivery Window:** 60-minute window provided

## Key Information Elements

### 1. Delivery Date and Time Window
The email specifies:
- Exact delivery date (e.g., "14th April 2026")
- One-hour delivery window (e.g., "10:13 - 11:13")
- These times are typically accurate within ±15 minutes

### 2. Driver Information
- Driver's first name is provided (e.g., "Paul")
- This is your DPD Local driver assignment
- Useful for coordinating with building security

### 3. Tracking Number
Format: **1597 6979 228 492** (16 digits in groups of 4)
- Use this to track parcel status in DPD system
- Required if raising delivery queries

## Delivery Options

If you cannot be present during the delivery window, the email provides an "options" link allowing you to:

- Redirect to alternative address
- Arrange safe place delivery
- Reschedule for another day
- Nominate a neighbour (residential addresses only)
- Collect from local DPD pickup point

**⚠️ WARNING:** Delivery option changes must be made at least 1 hour before the delivery window begins. Last-minute changes may not be processed in time.

## Action Steps for NBNE Operators

### Before Delivery

1. **Check the delivery window** against your site availability
2. **Notify reception/security** if at a managed facility
3. **Verify delivery address** matches your current site location
4. **Check equipment manifest** if expecting multiple Colourbyte items
5. **Prepare secure receiving area** for sensitive equipment

### During Delivery Window

1. **Ensure authorized personnel** are available to sign
2. **Have photo ID ready** (driver may request)
3. **Prepare to inspect packaging** for visible damage before signing
4. **Keep tracking number accessible** for confirmation

### After Delivery

1. **Photograph packaging** if damage is evident
2. **Report delivery** in Cairn using tracking number
3. **Inspect contents immediately** (Colourbyte equipment is time-sensitive)
4. **Log any discrepancies** within 24 hours

## Common Pitfalls

**❌ Ignoring delivery windows:** DPD operates on tight schedules. Missing your window may result in 24-48 hour delays for redelivery.

**❌ Incorrect site addresses:** Colourbyte orders sometimes default to billing address rather than delivery site. Always verify before delivery date.

**❌ Unsigned deliveries:** DPD requires signature for Colourbyte orders. "Leave in safe place" options may not be available for high-value equipment.

**❌ Not downloading proof of delivery:** If disputes arise, retrieve the signed POD from DPD website within 30 days.
