## Processing Steps

### 1. Review Attached Documents
- Check the attachment for exact quantities required
- Note specific sizes and dimensions
- Identify substrate specifications (aluminium panel, ACM, etc.)
- Check for any mounting/fixing requirements specified
- Review any durability or finish requirements

### 2. Site Survey Requirements
Berwick-upon-Tweed is a coastal town, so consider:
- Salt air exposure requiring marine-grade fixings
- Wind exposure for coastal locations
- Ground conditions for post installations
- Listed building or conservation area restrictions
- Planning permission status

### 3. Technical Specifications to Confirm
- **Substrate**: Aluminium composite, solid aluminium, or other?
- **Print method**: Digital print with anti-graffiti laminate standard
- **Mounting**: Post-mounted, wall-mounted, or lectern style?
- **Size**: Confirm dimensions from attachment
- **Quantity**: Verify number of units required
- **Locations**: Request site addresses for survey

### 4. Quotation Requirements
Include in your quote:
- Individual unit supply cost
- Installation costs per unit
- Site survey costs (or FOC if included)
- Groundworks/concrete bases if post-mounted
- Delivery to Northumberland
- Lead times for manufacture and installation
- Fixings specification (marine-grade stainless steel recommended)
- Warranty terms

### 5. Artwork Specifications
Request from client:
- File format (PDF, AI, or other vector format preferred)
- Resolution requirements if raster images included
- Colour profile information
- Any proofing requirements before production
- Number of proof rounds included in quote

## ⚠️ Warnings and Common Pitfalls

**Conservation Area**: Berwick-upon-Tweed has historic town walls and conservation areas. Check if planning permission is required and who is responsible for obtaining it.

**Coastal Environment**: Specify marine-grade fixings and appropriate substrate for longevity. Standard fixings will corrode rapidly.

**Client-Supplied Artwork**: Always include caveats about artwork quality and production-ready files. Build in proof stage and specify additional costs for artwork amendments.

**Ground Conditions**: Historic town may have buried archaeology, services, or cobbled streets. Include contingency for difficult installations.

**Access**: Check vehicle access to installation sites - historic town centres often have restricted access.

**Lead Times**: Be realistic about production and installation timescales, especially if planning permission required.
