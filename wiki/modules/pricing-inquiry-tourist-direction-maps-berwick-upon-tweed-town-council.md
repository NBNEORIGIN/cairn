## Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Attachment Assumptions**  
Don't assume "existing structure" means the frame is suitable. Check condition, compatibility with new panel specifications, and load-bearing capacity from site photo.

⚠️ **Coastal Environment**  
Berwick's coastal location means higher corrosion risk. Confirm marine-grade materials and fixings where appropriate.

⚠️ **Heritage Restrictions**  
Tourist signage in heritage towns often requires specific colours, materials, or styles. Verify any design restrictions with customer before finalising specification.

⚠️ **Ground Conditions**  
Historic town centres may have archaeological sensitivity or services below ground. Factor in potential for trial holes or service searches.

⚠️ **Artwork Timeline**  
Customer-supplied artwork can delay projects. Establish artwork deadline and format requirements upfront.

## Quotation Structure

When pricing, structure as:

1. **Option A:** Panel replacements only (all locations into existing structures)
2. **Option B:** Mixed scope (Location C replacement + other locations new units)
3. **Option C:** All new standalone units

Include separate line items for:
- Survey/site visit (if required)
- Supply (panels, frames, posts, fixings)
- Installation (segregated by type)
- Groundworks (if applicable)
- Traffic management
- Artwork installation
