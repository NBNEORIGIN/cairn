## Common Pitfalls

- **Assuming standard sizes for existing frames** - always verify measurements, even when "standard" sizes are mentioned
- **Starting production without written approval** - verbal or assumed approval creates timeline disputes
- **Not bundling related work** - banners should ideally be produced alongside main order for artwork consistency and batch efficiency
- **Unclear deposit vs balance terms** - ensure customer understands when balance is due (typically pre-delivery for events)
