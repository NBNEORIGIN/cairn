# AWS Summit London - Agentic AI, Innovation & Demos

AWS is hosting a summit on 22 April 2026 at Excel London featuring agentic AI, customer case studies, interactive demonstrations, and hands-on learning experiences. The event includes sessions on AI innovation, cloud technologies, and real-world applications from major organisations. Limited spaces are available and registration is required.

## Event Details

**Date:** 22 April 2026  
**Location:** Excel London  
**Cost:** Free (registration required)  
**Duration:** Full day event

### 1. Agentic AI & Innovation
The summit focuses on the next generation of AI systems that can reason, act, and deliver business value autonomously. Sessions will cover:
- Building and deploying agentic AI systems
- Scaling AI solutions in production environments
- Practical applications for business growth
- Direct access to AWS experts for implementation guidance
