# Pricing Tourist Direction Maps for Berwick-upon-Tweed Town Council

## Summary

This enquiry from Berwick-upon-Tweed Town Council concerns tourist direction maps at multiple locations. The client will supply artwork. Critical clarification was needed regarding which locations required full new units (frame + posts) versus panel replacements only. Client confirmed only Location C (Parade Car Park) attaches to an existing structure; all others require full new installations. This is a typical municipal project requiring careful scope definition before quoting.

## Key Warnings

⚠️ **Always clarify scope on mixed projects** - Never assume which locations need what type of installation. The cost implications are too significant.

⚠️ **Review attachment images** - The client provided an image of the Parade Car Park area. Review this before finalizing the quote to understand the existing structure and any site constraints.

⚠️ **Client-supplied artwork** - Note this in the quotation with appropriate caveats about file format, resolution, and approval process. Consider including artwork specification requirements in your response.

⚠️ **Municipal client** - Town councils typically require formal quotations and may have procurement procedures. Quote may need to be structured for PO matching.

⚠️ **Multiple locations in Berwick-upon-Tweed** - Site visits or coordination may be required. Factor in travel time/costs for installation. Consider if a single site visit can cover survey of all locations.

## Common Pitfalls

- **Under-quoting panel replacements** - Don't forget that even "simple" panel replacements may require access equipment, especially for tourist maps which are often in prominent locations
- **Overlooking groundworks** - New posts require appropriate foundations. Berwick is a historic town - check if any locations are in conservation areas requiring special permissions
- **Installation logistics** - Tourist maps in town centers may require parking permits, working hour restrictions, or traffic management
- **Wayfinding accuracy** - Tourist direction maps must be accurate. Clarify the approval process for client-supplied artwork and site-specific directional accuracy
