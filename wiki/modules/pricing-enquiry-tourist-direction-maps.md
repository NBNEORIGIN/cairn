# Pricing Enquiry - Tourist Direction Maps

## Summary
This enquiry from Berwick-upon-Tweed Town Council requests pricing for supply and installation of tourist direction maps. The customer will provide artwork, and has attached specifications including sizes and details. This is a straightforward external signage pricing request that requires a site visit to provide an accurate quotation.

## Classification
- **Enquiry Type:** Pricing request for supply & installation
- **Product Category:** Tourist information maps / wayfinding signage
- **Customer Type:** Local authority (Town Council)
- **Artwork Status:** Customer supplied
- **Stage:** Initial enquiry - requires quotation

## Required Actions

### 1. Acknowledge Receipt
- Respond promptly (within 4 business hours for new enquiries)
- Thank the customer for their enquiry
- Confirm you've received the attachment and are reviewing specifications
- Set expectations for quote turnaround time (typically 5-7 working days for projects requiring site visits)

### 2. Review Attachment
- Open and verify the specification document is readable
- Note exact sizes, quantities, and material requirements
- Check if substrate/mounting method is specified
- Identify any special requirements (e.g., anti-graffiti coating, lighting, accessibility considerations)
- If attachment is missing or unclear, request immediately

### 3. Site Assessment Requirements
For external wayfinding signage, you must establish:
- **Ground conditions** - Will posts need to be installed? What type of substrate?
- **Planning permissions** - Does the council already have approval? Who is responsible?
- **Access requirements** - Vehicle access for installation, parking restrictions
- **Existing infrastructure** - Mounting to existing posts/walls or new installation?
- **Quantities and locations** - How many maps and exact installation points

### 4. Information Gathering
Contact the customer to arrange:
- Site visit/survey (essential for accurate pricing)
- Clarification meeting if specifications are complex
- Discussion of timeline expectations

Questions to ask:
- How many individual map panels are required?
- Are installation locations confirmed?
- What is the desired installation method (post-mounted, wall-mounted, lectern-style)?
- What is the project timeline?
- Is there a budget range we should work within?
- Who handles planning permissions?

### 5. Pricing Components to Include
Your quotation should cover:
- **Materials:** Map panels (typically aluminium composite or similar durable substrate)
- **Printing/Production:** UV-resistant printing suitable for external use
- **Hardware:** Posts, mounting brackets, fixings (as required)
- **Installation:** Labour, transport, equipment hire
- **Site survey:** Include as separate line item if chargeable
- **Project management:** Especially if coordinating with customer's artwork timeline
- **Warranty/Maintenance:** Include terms

### 6. Quotation Submission
- Provide itemized breakdown
- Include material specifications and product lifespan
- State validity period (typically 30 days)
- Clarify exclusions (e.g., planning applications, groundworks beyond normal scope)
- Include examples or samples of similar work if available
- Attach relevant certifications or compliance documentation

## Warnings and Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Don't price blind** - External signage pricing without a site visit often leads to underquoting. Ground conditions, access issues, and existing infrastructure significantly impact installation costs.

⚠️ **Artwork liability** - Since customer is supplying artwork, include clear terms about file formats required, approval process, and that NBNE is not responsible for content accuracy or quality issues with supplied artwork.

⚠️ **Planning permissions** - Clarify who is responsible for obtaining necessary permissions. Local authorities usually handle this themselves, but confirm in writing.

⚠️ **Material specifications** - Tourist maps are permanent external installations. Ensure materials specified are weather-resistant and suitable for minimum 5-year lifespan. Avoid underspecifying to meet a price point.

⚠️ **Installation timing** - Consider seasonal factors. Winter ground conditions may affect post installation. Build buffer time into quotes.

⚠️ **Maintenance access** - Consider ongoing maintenance requirements and access for future updates.
