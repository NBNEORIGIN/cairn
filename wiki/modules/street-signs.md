# Street Signs

## Summary
Street signs are custom-manufactured products typically ordered by residents for private roads or developments. NBNE offers multiple construction options ranging from cost-effective aluminium composite to premium fabricated aluminium. Orders require a 25% deposit, visual approval before manufacture, and follow a standard specification process. This article covers the order workflow, specification options, pricing structure, and payment handling based on typical customer interactions.

## Product Specifications

### Standard Street Sign Components
- **Lettering height:** 90mm (standard UK specification)
- **Lettering colour:** Black
- **Background:** White
- **Typeface:** Kindersely Sans (most commonly used UK street sign font)
- **Border:** Black applied border
- **Fixing:** Pre-drilled for wall mounting
- **Optional additions:** T-junction symbol (coloured "T") positioned right-hand side

### Construction Options

**Option 1: Aluminium Composite Panel (Cost-Effective)**
- Size: Approx. 600–750mm × 150mm (scaled to suit layout)
- Construction: Aluminium composite panel
- Finish: Black applied lettering and border
- Price: £200 + VAT for both signs (street sign + T-junction)
- Best for: Budget-conscious customers, residential developments

**Option 2: Fabricated Aluminium (Premium)**
- Street sign size: 600–750mm wide × 150mm high
- T-junction sign: 150mm square
- Construction: Folded aluminium tray, powder-coated black
- Face: White panel with applied lettering
- Lettering: Individual aluminium letters, powder-coated black
- Border: Integrated aluminium frame
- Price: £295 + VAT (street sign) + £195 + VAT (T-junction sign)
- Best for: Customers wanting closest match to traditional council-style signs, maximum durability

## Order Workflow

### Step 1: Initial Enquiry
- Customer contacts with requirements (street name, preferred style)
- Offer site visit if customer wants to view samples at workshop
- Provide specification options and pricing

### Step 2: Quotation
- Confirm exact street name spelling (critical—verify carefully)
- Specify construction option selected
- Provide detailed specification summary customer can share with neighbours/co-purchasers
- State pricing clearly: individual item costs or combined price
- Confirm deposit requirement: **25% of total order value**

### Step 3: Deposit Payment
Customer has two payment options:
- Bank transfer (preferred)
- Card payment over phone

**To proceed with deposit:**
1. Raise deposit invoice (25% of quoted total)
2. Customer provides contact number if paying by phone
3. Confirm payment received before proceeding to design

### Step 4: Design Approval
- Prepare visual layout showing exact sign appearance
- Submit to customer for approval
- **DO NOT begin manufacture without written approval**
- Allow customer to share layout with neighbours/co-purchasers if applicable

## Warnings & Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Street Name Spelling:** Always verify exact spelling and formatting. Customers may use incorrect capitalisation or spacing. Confirm in writing.

⚠️ **No Design Work Before Order:** Do not prepare visual mockups or detailed layouts until deposit is received. Specification descriptions and pricing only at quotation stage.

⚠️ **Neighbour Consultations:** Customers often need to consult multiple residents. Provide clear written specifications they can easily share. Expect delays in decision-making for shared orders.

⚠️ **Deposit Required:** 25% deposit is mandatory to proceed. Do not begin design work on good faith.

⚠️ **Supply Only:** Standard offering is collection from workshop. Customer arranges own installation. Confirm customer understands this.

## Pricing Notes

- Fabricated aluminium charged per individual sign component
- Deposit: always 25% of total order value
