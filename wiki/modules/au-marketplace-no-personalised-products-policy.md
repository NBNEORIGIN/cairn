# AU Marketplace — No Personalised Products Policy

## Policy

NBNE has **never listed personalised products on the Amazon AU marketplace**. This is a standing operational rule, not an oversight.

## Data confirmation

Checked against `ami_sku_mapping` (as at May 2026):

- Total AU SKUs: **353**
- Personalised AU SKUs: **0**

No exceptions exist across the full mapping table.

## Implications for Deek

- When analysing phase-outs or delistings of personalised SKUs, **do not raise AU as an open question** — it is always N/A.
- AU absence from personalised product listings is never a deficit, gap, or anomaly.
- This rule applies blanket to all M-numbers where `is_personalised = true`.

Confirmed by Toby Fletcher, May 2026. Verified by direct query against `ami_sku_mapping`.
