## Warnings and Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Link Expiration:** Calendly cancellation and rescheduling links are unique to each booking. Do not share these links with unauthorized individuals, as they provide direct access to modify the meeting.

⚠️ **Location Selection Error:** Only select "Your Office Address" if it truly represents your usual location. Selecting this option when it doesn't apply may cause confusion about the actual meeting venue.

⚠️ **Last-Minute Changes:** Making changes too close to the scheduled meeting time may not provide adequate notice to other participants. Follow NBNE protocols for minimum notice periods when rescheduling.

⚠️ **Communication Gap:** Calendly handles scheduling mechanics but may not convey context about why meetings are cancelled or rescheduled. Always follow up with direct communication to other attendees explaining any changes.

## Best Practices

### Before the Meeting

1. **Respond Promptly:** Select your preferred time within 24-48 hours of receiving the invitation
2. **Verify Location:** Confirm the meeting location matches your understanding with other attendees
3. **Calendar Sync:** Ensure the meeting appears in your primary calendar system
4. **Prepare Materials:** Review meetings typically require documentation or progress reports

### If Changes Are Needed

1. **Assess Urgency:** Determine if the change is necessary or if the original time can still work
2. **Check Availability:** Before rescheduling, verify alternative times work for all parties
3. **Communicate Proactively:** Send a direct message to other attendees explaining the need for changes
4. **Use Appropriate Action:** Cancel only if the meeting is no longer needed; reschedule if you need a different time

### Meeting Follow-Up

1. Document meeting outcomes in the appropriate NBNE knowledge management system
2. Update any action items in your task tracking system
3. Share meeting notes with attendees who were unable to attend
