# Northumberland Small Business Service Facebook Group Notifications

This article covers Facebook notification emails from the Northumberland Small Business Service group, which promote local networking events and business opportunities in Northumberland. These automated emails are sent to the toby@nbnesigns.com address when new posts are made to the group. Understanding these notifications helps NBNE operators identify relevant local business networking opportunities and manage communication preferences.

## Email Structure

Facebook group notification emails from Northumberland Small Business Service typically contain:

1. **Subject Line**: "Northumberland Small Business Service posted in Northumberland Small Business Service"
2. **View on Facebook Link**: Direct link to the original post
3. **Post Content**: The actual message shared in the group (quoted text)
4. **Footer Information**: Unsubscribe options and Meta legal information

## Identifying Networking Events

When reviewing these notifications, look for:

1. **Event Type**: "Northumberland Networking" events at various locations
2. **Date and Time**: Specific event dates (e.g., "Wednesday 15th April" with start time "09:30")
3. **Location**: Venue details (e.g., "Cheviot Centre" in Wooler)
4. **Registration Links**: URLs to northumberlandsmallbusiness.co.uk for free event registration
5. **Hashtags**: Common tags include #NorthEastCA, #Northumberland, #Networking

## Common Event Details

Northumberland Networking events typically offer:

- **Cost**: FREE networking
- **Timing**: Morning sessions (09:30 start is common)
- **Refreshments**: Breakfast sandwiches and hot beverages ("cuppa")
- **Format**: Informal business networking
- **Rotating Locations**: Different venues across Northumberland

## Managing These Notifications

### Option 1: Continue Receiving Notifications
If these events are valuable for NBNE's local business presence, continue receiving notifications and:

1. Review emails weekly for upcoming events
2. Add relevant events to company calendar
3. Determine if attendance aligns with business development goals
4. Register using the provided links

### Option 2: Adjust Notification Settings
To reduce email volume without leaving the group:

1. Click "View on Facebook" link in any notification
2. Navigate to the group settings on Facebook
3. Adjust notification preferences to "Highlights" or "Off"
4. Remain a group member for manual checking

### Option 3: Unsubscribe Completely
Use the unsubscribe link provided at the bottom of the email if these notifications are not relevant to NBNE operations.

## ⚠️ Warnings and Common Pitfalls

**Date Discrepancies**: The example email shows a 2026 date in the registration URL, which may indicate:
- Template/placeholder content
- Copy-paste errors in the original post
- Always verify actual event dates before planning attendance

**Email Security**: 
- The footer warns "please don't forward this email" for security reasons
- Links contain user-specific tokens tied to toby@nbnesigns.com
- Do not share these emails externally or forward to non-NBNE addresses

**Link Validity**:
- Facebook notification links contain time-sensitive tokens
- Links may expire after a period
- If a link doesn't work, search for the event directly on the Northumberland Small Business Service Facebook page or website

**Recipient Address**:
- These emails are sent to toby@nbnesigns.com (note: nbnesigns.com, not originjewellery.co.uk)
- The body mentions "Hi Jo" - verify if this is personalized or generic greeting
- Ensure the correct team member monitors this inbox
