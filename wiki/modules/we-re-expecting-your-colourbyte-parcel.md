# We're Expecting Your Colourbyte Parcel

This article covers the standard DPD delivery notification process for Colourbyte parcels sent to NBNE operators. When you receive this email, it means DPD has been notified of an incoming shipment but hasn't yet physically received it. Understanding this notification helps you plan for delivery and avoid common communication issues with suppliers or the logistics team.

## What This Email Means

The "We're expecting your parcel" notification is **pre-delivery confirmation**, not a dispatch notification. DPD has received electronic shipping data from Colourbyte but does not yet have physical possession of the parcel. You should not expect immediate delivery.

**Tracking Number Format:** 1597 6979 228 492 (16-digit DPD reference)

## Expected Timeline

1. **Day 0 (Email received):** DPD notification system activated; parcel may still be at sender's facility
2. **Day 1-3:** Parcel collected from Colourbyte and processed through DPD sorting network
3. **Day of delivery:** 1-hour delivery window sent by 12:00pm (typically)
4. **Delivery attempt:** Photo taken at doorway instead of signature

> **⚠️ WARNING:** The email states "out for delivery" but this is misleading template language. The parcel is NOT yet out for delivery when you receive this initial notification. Wait for the 1-hour time slot notification before making yourself available.

## Delivery Method

DPD uses **contactless photo delivery** for Colourbyte shipments:

- No signature required
- Driver photographs parcel in your open doorway
- Photo serves as proof of delivery
- You must be available to receive (cannot be left unattended)

## Tracking Your Parcel

### Using the Tracking Number

1. Visit DPD tracking portal or download DPD app
2. Enter tracking number: **1597 6979 228 492** format
3. Monitor status updates until "loaded on van" notification received
4. Wait for 1-hour delivery window (usually sent by 12:00pm on delivery day)

### DPD App Features

- Real-time delivery updates
- GPS tracking of delivery van
- Delivery preference management
- Redirect to Pickup Point option

## Delivery Options

You can modify delivery preferences through the "Show my options" link in the email:

- **Redirect to neighbour:** Specify alternative recipient
- **Redirect to Pickup Point:** Reduces CO₂ by 63% if collecting on foot/cycle
- **Reschedule delivery:** Choose alternative date
- **Leave in safe place:** Specify secure location (if available for your address)

> **⚠️ NOTE:** Not all options may be available for Colourbyte parcels. Some items require direct handover due to value or nature of contents. Check with IT Asset Management if unsure.

## Common Pitfalls

### 1. **Waiting at Home Too Early**
Operators often stay home all day after receiving this email. The parcel may not arrive for 2-3 days. Only clear your schedule when you receive the 1-hour time slot notification.

### 2. **Assuming Delivery Failed**
If you don't receive a time slot by 12:00pm, check tracking status. The parcel may still be in transit or delayed. Don't log a ticket until tracking shows "delivery attempted."

### 3. **Missing the Delivery Window**
The 1-hour window is tight. Set phone alerts and monitor the DPD app if you've downloaded it. Missed deliveries create 24-48 hour delays.

### 4. **Contacting Colourbyte Prematurely**
Wait at least 5 working days from this initial notification before escalating to the supplier. DPD's standard service is 3-5 days from collection.

## When to Escalate

Contact IT Asset Management if:

- No tracking updates for 5+ working days
- Tracking shows "delivery attempted" but you were available
- Parcel redirected without your authorization
- Photo proof of delivery doesn't match your location

Include your tracking number (1597 6979 228 492 format) in all correspondence.

## Environmental Considerations

DPD promotes Pickup Point collection citing 63% CO₂ reduction per parcel when collected on foot or cycle. For non-urgent Colourbyte deliveries, consider this option if:

- Pickup Point is within walking distance
- Delivery timing is flexible
- You work from multiple locations

This calculation assumes collection without vehicle use and accounts for final-mile delivery emissions.
