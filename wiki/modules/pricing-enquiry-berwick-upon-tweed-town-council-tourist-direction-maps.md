## Scope Clarification Process

### Initial Enquiry Response

When receiving specifications for multi-location sign projects, always clarify the installation scope before pricing:

1. **Identify potential mixed scope** - PO descriptions may include both new units and replacement panels
2. **Request explicit confirmation** on which locations need:
   - Full new units (frame + posts + foundations)
   - Panel replacements only (into existing structures)
3. **Wait for customer confirmation** before proceeding with quotation

### Customer Confirmation Received

**Key information from customer response:**

- **Location C - Parade Car Park:** Panel replacement into existing structure only
- **All other locations:** Full new units required (inferred from "only C... is attaching to an existing structure")
- **Site image provided:** Customer attached image of Location C area

## Quotation Structuring

### Pricing Breakdown

Structure the quote to clearly separate:

1. **Full New Units** (Locations A, B, D, E, etc.)
   - Frame/display case
   - Post(s) and fixings
   - Foundation works
   - Installation labour
   - Pricing per unit based on size specification

2. **Panel Replacement** (Location C only)
   - Panel only
   - Mounting/fixing to existing structure
   - Installation labour
   - Reduced pricing vs full unit

### Cost-Effective Approach

- Price panel replacements significantly lower than full units
- Consider economies of scale for multiple full units
- Factor in single site visit for all locations if geographically clustered
- Account for travel to Northumberland location

## Important Warnings

> ⚠️ **Do not assume all locations are the same scope** - Always clarify new vs replacement before quoting. Incorrect assumptions lead to:
> - Underquoted prices (quoting panel-only when full unit needed)
> - Overquoted prices (quoting full units when panels sufficient)
> - Customer dissatisfaction and quote revisions

> ⚠️ **Existing structure assessment required** - For Location C (Parade Car Park), confirm:
> - Existing structure is suitable for new panel mounting
> - Panel dimensions are compatible with existing frame
> - Consider site survey if structure condition unknown

> ⚠️ **Customer-supplied artwork considerations:**
> - Request artwork in advance to verify print suitability
> - Confirm file format, resolution, and colour specifications
> - Include artwork approval stage in quotation timeline
> - Clarify liability for artwork errors/quality issues

## Common Pitfalls

- **Not reading customer responses carefully** - "Only C" means all others are opposite
- **Quoting without size specifications** - Wait for full spec document before pricing
- **Forgetting location access considerations** - Town centre vs car park may affect installation timing/methodology
- **Not accounting for council processes** - Local authority projects often require specific documentation, permissions, or timescales
