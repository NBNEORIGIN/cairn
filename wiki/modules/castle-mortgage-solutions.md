## Customer Decision Points

### Depth Reduction
Customer confirmed acceptable to reduce sign depth from 50mm to 40mm. This likely relates to:
- Site constraints
- Mounting surface limitations
- Planning or building regulations
- Cost optimization

**Warning:** Always confirm depth reductions in writing. Verify that reduction doesn't compromise structural integrity or visual impact expectations.

### Face Type Selection
Customer chose flush face over 3D lettering, prioritizing:
- Lower upfront cost (£145 saving)
- Robust construction
- Simpler maintenance

## Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Depth specifications:** Customer accepted 40mm but original was 50mm. Ensure manufacturing team uses correct 40mm specification, not original measurement.

⚠️ **Installation clarity:** Confirm installation inclusion/cost for the selected £480 option. Don't assume it matches the 3D option terms.

⚠️ **Website branding:** Customer provided website link. Check site for:
- Current logo version and colours
- Brand guidelines
- Pantone/colour specifications
- Font usage

⚠️ **Tray sign substrate:** Confirm tray material, backing, and whether customer expects illumination (not mentioned but verify at design stage).
