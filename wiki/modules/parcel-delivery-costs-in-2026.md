## Evaluation Checklist

When considering this or similar carrier proposals:

1. **Verify performance claims**
   - Request case studies or references from similar businesses
   - Ask for Service Level Agreement (SLA) documentation
   - Confirm what happens when the 98.5% delivery rate isn't met

2. **Compare total costs**
   - Base shipping rates
   - Surcharges (fuel, residential, remote area, etc.)
   - International fees and customs handling charges
   - Failed delivery or return costs

3. **Assess service requirements**
   - Does 24/48hr meet customer expectations?
   - Is six-day delivery sufficient, or do customers need Sunday delivery?
   - What integration work is needed with current e-commerce platform?

4. **Review contract terms**
   - Minimum volume commitments
   - Contract length and exit clauses
   - Price increase mechanisms
   - Liability limitations

## Warnings and Common Pitfalls

⚠️ **Don't share detailed current pricing without approval** - This information is commercially sensitive and should only be shared after internal discussion and authorization from appropriate stakeholders.

⚠️ **Evri's reputation** - Evri (formerly Hermes UK) has historically had mixed customer reviews. Research current customer satisfaction ratings and complaints data before committing to large volumes.

⚠️ **Hidden costs** - Initial quotes may not include all surcharges. Request a comprehensive breakdown including all potential additional fees.

⚠️ **Integration costs** - Switching carriers often requires IT integration work, label printing changes, and staff retraining. Factor these costs into any comparison.

⚠️ **Volume commitments** - Sales representatives may offer attractive rates contingent on minimum volumes. Ensure any commitments are realistic and sustainable.
